## Configure Your Numbers

In this section, youâ€™ll learn how to **set up** or **import** phone numbers into your Callin.io platform. These numbers are essential for enabling outbound and inbound call capabilities for your AI agents.

### Option 1: Purchase a Number

1. From the left sidebar, go to **Numbers**.
2. Click the **Purchase Number** button at the top.
3. A popup will appear where you can:
   - Select your **country**.
   - Choose the number type: **Local Number** or **Toll-Free Number**.
   - Optionally, specify an **area code**.
4. Click **Search Available Numbers**.
5. Select a number and click **Purchase Selected Number**.

### Option 2: Import from Twilio

1. Click the **Import from Twilio** button.
2. Enter your **Twilio Account SID** and **Twilio Auth Token**.
3. Click **Search Twilio Numbers** to retrieve and import your existing numbers.

Once added, the numbers will be available for assignment to your agents and campaigns.
